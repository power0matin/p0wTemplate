# Neo Minimal Theme

For those who love white space, stark contrasts, and absolute simplicity.

## Aesthetics
- **High Contrast**: Harsh black and white tones with zero gradients.
- **Oversized Typography**: Uses bold, massive numbers to instantly communicate traffic stats to the user.
- **Stripped Down**: Removes all unnecessary borders, shadows, and decorations.

## Features
- **Instant Clarity**: Users immediately see their traffic remaining in massive font.
- **Quick Actions Pill**: Floating button at the bottom right for instant configuration imports.
- **Dark/Light Mode**: Flips the stark contrast beautifully depending on OS preferences.
